Tiger

In 2016, Tiger numbers went up for the first time in decades

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 250 to 390 cm
  • Average Lifespace: Item #3
  • Habitat: places that support large appropriately sized prey

Fur is a deep orange with horizonal black stripes all over, as fur nears the belly it becomes white. It is the largest cat species in the world, and resides all over the the eurasian continent. They are most numerous in India.
